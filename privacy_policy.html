Privacy Policy -- iCard Store

Last Updated: February 17th, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Card Storage ("the App") is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party.

Data Collection

iCard Store does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside of your device. All app functionality is performed locally on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Card metadata** (bank name, card type, expiry date, notes, groups, reminders): stored in Core Data on your device.
  • Sensitive card data** (card number, CVV code, PIN): stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain, protected by hardware encryption.

    • iCloud Sync

    If you are signed into iCloud on your device, card metadata (but never card numbers or CVV codes) may be synced between your own Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is private and managed entirely by Apple. The developer has no visibility into or access to this data.

    Data the Developer Does NOT Collect

    • No analytics or usage statistics
    • No crash reporting sent to third-party services
    • No advertising identifiers
    • No location data
    • No personal identification information
    • No card numbers, CVV codes, or PINs â€” ever

      • Permissions Used

      Permission Purpose

      • Face ID / Touch ID:To authenticate you before showing card data
      • Camera:To optionally scan a physical card (no image is stored)
      • Notifications:To send you card expiry and payment reminders
      • Reminders:To optionally create reminders in your Apple Reminders app

        • Data Deletion

        Deleting the App from your device removes all locally stored Core Data records. Keychain items associated with the App are also removed. iCloud data associated with the App is removed when you delete the App from all devices or disable iCloud Drive.

        Children's Privacy

        The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
